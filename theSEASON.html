general

this a soccer game, with 2 players in a team, no goalkeeper, and only 1 minute of game-time per match. you are in control of the team, 'slate'. 'slate' is of a greyish color. you are competing against 3 other teams.

controls

use the arrow keys to move your player, up, down, left and right respectively. press 'z' to pass the ball to your teamate, and 'x' to shoot the ball towards the goal.

the player that moves according to your controls is the player that is currently targeted.
